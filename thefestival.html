THE FESTIVAL

Immerse yourself in the vibrant experience of the Onda Festival, where the word "onda" comes to life through a unique musical journey. This festival celebrates the diversity of genres and styles, merging melodies that ripple through the vast musical universe.

Onda is more than an event ; it's a celebration of the creative energy that flows like an expansive wave, connecting music lovers in a sonic communion. From gentle acoustic waves to powerful electronic currents, each performance is an ode to the multiplicity of musical expressions.

Add your energy to the tide of sounds that will flood the stage, experiencing the magic of waves intertwining and converging in an auditory ecstasy. Get ready to immerse yourself in the Onda and let the musical current carry you away!

At Onda, we want every festival-goer to have the best possible experience during the four days of the festival. We are committed to taking responsibility for combating all forms of discrimination, harassment and violence, and we are determined to minimise our environmental impact.

By visiting the Onda site, you are aware of the values of the festival and you undertake to respect this charter of good conduct. Let's work together to ensure that Onda remains a caring, inclusive, safe and environmentally-friendly venue! Onda also attaches particular importance to limiting its impact on the environment. The whole festival team is committed to a collective responsible approach and to persevering year after year.

It's with all these small actions that, together, we'll be able to achieve our big commitments and make our festival a better place.
